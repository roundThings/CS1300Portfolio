ABOUT

Hi, I'm SquareThings!

I'm a junior at Brown University studying cognitive science and HCI who is perpetually curious about the novel ways in which design is shaping the world we are in. I love finding creative applications of emerging technology that can be used to improve our everyday life.

My Process

Identify and contextualize user challenges to understand how they fit into a larger pattern of behavior.
Iterate rapidly, collabratively, and strategically -and always annotate!
Narrow down the key deliverables for each stakeholder segment and create a communication strategy for effective sharebacks.
